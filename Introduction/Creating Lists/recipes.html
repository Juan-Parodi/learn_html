Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 TableSpoons of Wired Brain Coffee hot cocoa mix

  • 5 Marshmellows

Steps

  1. Scoop cocoa mix into the cup
  2. Add milk slowly, while stirring the cup
  3. Add marshmellows
